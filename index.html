About Me

I am an associate research professor at the Steward Observatory at the University of Arizona and a member of the JWST/Mid-Infrared Instrument (MIRI) team.

My research is focused on disentangling the internal and external drivers of galaxy evolution
from our perspective of the infrared Universe. I specialize in studies of galaxy clusters, cosmic dust, and Active Galactic Nuclei.
